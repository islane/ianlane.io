About

I am a web developer with a diverse background spanning marketing, design, and IT. Through my experience, I am able to devise innovative solutions across the entire stack. On the front-end, I am passionate about building sites that are user-friendly, responsive, and attractive. As a back-end developer, I am always striving to build upon my knowledge and learn new languages, libraries, and frameworks. Professionally, I most often work with sites built around PHP-based content management systems and Ruby on Rails.
